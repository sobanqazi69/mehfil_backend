Delete Your Account

Mehfil — last updated 16 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your Mehfil account (app ID: com.mehfil.microdesk) and exactly what happens to your data when you do.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us from the same email address you signed in with (this is how we verify the request is really yours).
  2. Use the subject line “Delete my account”.
  3. Send it to orbenotechnologies@gmail.com.

Email us to delete my account

We action every request within 30 days of receiving it, and email you to confirm once it is done. You do not need to give a reason.

What gets deleted

DataWhat happens
Your account (Google ID, name, email)Permanently deleted
Your username and bioPermanently deleted
Your profile photo (if you uploaded one)File permanently deleted from our server
Chat messages you sentPermanently deleted
Your room memberships and historyPermanently deleted
Rooms you createdPermanently deleted

What we do not hold

We never record or store voice audio, so there is nothing to delete there. We do not collect your location or contacts, and we do not sell data to anyone. See our Privacy Policy for the full picture.

Data we keep, and for how long

Once your account is deleted we do not retain any personal data about you. Server logs that may incidentally contain an IP address are rotated and discarded within 30 days as part of normal operation, and are not linked back to your account after deletion.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you sign in again afterwards, a brand-new empty account is created — your old rooms, messages and profile cannot be restored.
